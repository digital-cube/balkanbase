BASE jumping, also sometimes written as B.A.S.E jumping, is an activity that employs an initially packed
parachute to jump from fixed objects. "B.A.S.E." is an acronym that stands for four categories of fixed
objects from which one can jump: buildings, antennas, spans (bridges), and earth (cliffs).
